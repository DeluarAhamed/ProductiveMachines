## SEO note

Google does not guarantee first position rankings. This implementation follows Search Central fundamentals: help search engines understand the content, keep pages crawlable, use descriptive titles/snippets, connect pages with useful links, provide sitemaps/canonicals, and add structured data where it accurately describes the page.
